## 🚨 KEY FINDINGS

### 1. **Widespread Misinformation**
- Many websites incorrectly list Walmart, Kohl's, Best Buy as having military discounts
- These chains have explicitly stated they do NOT offer standard military discounts
- Verification requires checking official corporate sites, not third-party discount aggregators

### 2. **Veterans Day vs Year-Round**
- Most restaurants offer Veterans Day specials ONLY, not year-round discounts
- Only Outback Steakhouse offers consistent year-round restaurant discount
- Many "discounts" are actually one-day annual events

### 3. **Franchise vs Corporate**
- Fast food chains (Subway, Arby's) leave discounts to individual franchise owners
- Results in inconsistent availability and no corporate policy
- Creates confusion for military customers

### 4. **Location Dependencies**
- Buffalo Wild Wings: "Participating locations only"
- Hotels: Rates vary significantly by individual property
- Entertainment: Often only at select locations

### 5. **Verification Requirements**
- Many chains require ID.me verification
- Military ID always required for in-person discounts
- Some have complex restrictions (exclusions, limits, etc.)
